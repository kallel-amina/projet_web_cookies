Our Commitment to You

At Sweet Bites, the aroma of fresh pastries is the only thing we want lingering. We are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our visitors. This Privacy Policy explains clearly and without legal jargon how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or order our artisanal treats. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

folder_shared Information We Collect

Personal Identification

Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address provided during checkout. We need this to make sure your pastries arrive at the right door.

Payment Information

Encrypted credit card details. These are processed securely by our third-party payment providers (Stripe/PayPal) and are never stored on our servers.

Technical Data

IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior collected via cookies to help us improve your shopping experience.

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To contact you: To contact you by email, telephone calls, SMS, or other equivalent forms of electronic communication regarding updates or informative communications.

cookie Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our service and store certain information. Tracking technologies used are beacons, tags, and scripts to collect and track information and to improve and analyze our service.

Can I disable cookies?

Yes. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some parts of our service.
